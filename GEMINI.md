# Project Mission: Couche-Tard Growth Engine

**Objective:** Increase fuel-to-food conversion rates by 15% via the Inner Circle loyalty program.

---

## 1. Who is Alimentation Couche-Tard?

*   **Global Footprint:** Founded in 1980 in Laval, Quebec, Couche-Tard has grown from a single store into a global retail leader with approximately **16,700 stores** across **31 countries**.
*   **Primary Brands:**
    *   **Couche-Tard:** The flagship brand in Quebec, recognizable by its red winking owl mascot.
    *   **Circle K:** The global brand (present in 27+ countries), serving as the primary face of the company in the US and the rest of Canada.
    *   **Ingo:** Automated fuel-only sites in Europe.
*   **Business Model:** A **"decentralized-centralized"** model that empowers local managers to tailor merchandise while leveraging global procurement scale.

## 2. Core Strategic Pillars (2026 "Core + More")

*   **Amplify the Core:** Focus on high-frequency, high-margin categories: **Fuel, Nicotine, and Beverages** (the "Thirst" category).
*   **Food Journey:** A strategic push to transform convenience stores into dining destinations through **'Fresh Food Fast'** and the recent integration of GetGo Café + Market assets.
*   **Fuel-to-Food Conversion:** Fuel accounts for **~75% of total revenue** and acts as the primary traffic driver. Our mission is to convert these forecourt visitors into high-margin in-store shoppers using the **Inner Circle** loyalty program and the **Horizon AI platform** for personalized rewards.

## 3. Operational Aesthetic & Brand Persona

*   **Keywords:** Fast, Clean, Friendly, Energetic, and Easy.
*   **Value Proposition:** *"Easy to Visit, Easy to Buy"*
*   **Signature SKUs:** Polar Pop (Circle K), Fresh Food Fast (Pizza/Sandwiches), and Premium Dark Roast Coffee.
*   **Tone of Voice:** Professional, retail-centric, and focused on "Basket Expansion" and "Customer Friction Reduction."
