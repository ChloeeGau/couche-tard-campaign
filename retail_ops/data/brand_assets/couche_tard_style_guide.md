# Couche-Tard Brand Style Guide

## Visual Identity
- **Logo Variants:**
    - Couche-Tard Owl Logo (Iconic Red Winking Owl).
    - Couche-Tard Long Version (Logotype with text).
    - Combined Couche-Tard Circle K Logo (For co-branded assets).
- **Color Palette:** 'Owl Red' (#E31837) as primary.

## Brand Mantra
"Place aux moments qui comptent" (Make room for moments that matter).

## Philosophy
Focus on local relevance, convenience, and friendly service.
